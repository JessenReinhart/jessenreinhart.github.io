# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, engineering managers, and Indonesian SME owners scanning a senior frontend portfolio for proof of delivery at scale (banks, ecommerce, high-traffic products). Context: cold visit on desktop or mobile, 30–90 seconds, bilingual EN/ID.

## Product Purpose

Convert a short visit into a hire or project inquiry. Surface credible outcomes (Action→Result), selected work, and a clear contact path without forcing a call.

## Brand Personality

Fast, open, precise. Mirror's Edge runner energy: bright planes, one red signal, kinetic motion, no luxury gloom.

## Anti-references

- Dark monochrome "dev terminal" portfolios
- Gold/luxury gamification dashboards
- Glassmorphism-everywhere SaaS templates
- AI-purple gradients, three equal feature cards, custom luxury cursors as the hero

## Design Principles

1. **Signal, not decoration** — one Faith red accent for interactive value only.
2. **Air and speed** — large negative space, hard edges, directional motion over floaty fades.
3. **Proof over poetry** — stats and work lead; jargon last.
4. **Bilingual parity** — EN/ID stay in sync; no layout that only works for one language.
5. **Preserve the contract** — section IDs, scroll-spy, resume print, and data content stay stable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on light primary and dark secondary. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for all parkour motion. Keyboard-reachable nav, focus rings in accent red.
